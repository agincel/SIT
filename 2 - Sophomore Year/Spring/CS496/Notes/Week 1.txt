Expressions: Atomic or Composite

Atomic - collapses to itself (an int, a boolean, etc) (also called values)
Composite - collapses to a value, like 5 + 7

Symbols: atomic values that print like identifiers
Must be preceded with a quote and start with an identifier

> 'a
'a

> 'hello
'hello
